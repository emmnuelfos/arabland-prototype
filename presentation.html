The Brief
Chapter 01 — Understanding

Not a portal.
A boutique.

Concept Plus represents a small, deliberately senior book — Palm villas, Burj-view penthouses, Emirates Hills estates. The brief asked for a website that feels less like a search engine and more like a private viewing.

We started by removing things — banners, filters, pop-ups, every portal reflex — until what remained was the property, the broker, and the address.

Design Principles
Chapter 02 — North Star

Three principles. Repeated everywhere.

01

Curation over volume.

A handful of properties shown beautifully will always outperform a thousand shown in a row.

02

Discretion over noise.

No pop-ups, no urgency timers, no portal patterns. The page does not chase — it waits.

03

Craft over template.

Hairlines, baseline grids, considered numerals. Every detail is held to the same standard the property holds itself to.

Adnika × Concept Plus 08 / 14
Functionality
Chapter 08 — Interactions

Everywhere.
Never in the way.

Twelve considered interactions that compound across the entire site. Each one persists across pages — a shortlist drawer that travels with the user, a currency setting that respects intent, a compare tray that quietly stacks.

01

Photo Lightbox

Full-bleed gallery with thumbnail rail, keyboard nav and pinch zoom.

02

Mortgage Calculator

Live amortization with LTV, term and rate scrubbing. UAE + offshore.

03

Live Valuation

Per-sqft engine with confidence band, refined by a senior broker in 24h.

04

Shortlist Drawer

Persistent across pages, share by link, send-to-broker in one click.

05

Compare Tray

Side-by-side up to four properties, with mortgage and yield columns.

06

Schedule a Viewing

Calendar-style picker tied to the broker on the listing.

07

Payment Plan

Five-milestone off-plan timeline with reservation fees breakdown.

08

Stylized Map

Custom illustrated Dubai map — feels printed, not tracked.

09

Currency · Area · Lang

AED / USD / EUR, sqft / sqm, EN / AR — persistent across sessions.

10

Filter Bar

Sticky chips for community, type, beds and price — quietly persistent.

11

Agent Drawer

Broker profile with active mandates, call, WhatsApp, email — all in-page.

12

Apply with CV

Position-detail apply flow with file attach and confidential submission.
